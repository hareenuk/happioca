Our Story

At Happioca, we believe that food isn't just about filling your tummy. It's about creating moments.

We bring you the authentic aroma of local tea and coffee, paired with our signature tapioca-based dishes. All served in an ambience designed to make you relax, laugh, and create memories.

Why Happioca?

Aromatic sips

From the earthy smell of freshly brewed tea to the bold richness of local coffee, our drinks taste like home.

Cozy ambience

A warm, rustic space designed for laughter, conversations, and little escapes from the everyday rush.

Built with passion

Every detail at Happioca carries the heart of the five dreamers who started this journey.
